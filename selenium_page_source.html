About us

National Dental Care, Brisbane (CBD) is a well-established metro practice. Servicing the residents of Brisbane for over 100 years, this practice can be traced back to the roots of first Principal, Mr Sagar; in 1902, on the corner of Queen Street and Albert Street, Brisbane. Providing an inner city solution to all your oral health needs, National Dental Care, Brisbane (CBD) is principally tailored for the busy, corporate and inner city residents. National Dental Care, Brisbane (CBD) offers a full suite of services from Cosmetic and Preventative Dentistry to General Dentistry, including Invisalign - the clear alternative to braces. Located in the heart of Brisbane on Queen Street, parking is available in the nearby Myer Centre for our patients' convenience.

    Priya obtained her Bachelor of Dental Science from King's College London and worked in the United Kingdom for several years. Priya is very passionate about dentistry, and she has a strong focus on providing the best possible dental care to her patients, with a gentle and caring approach. She considers the impact that poor oral health has on the whole body and likes to ensure that her patients fully understand all options that are available to them. Priya has specific interests in restorative, cosmetic, periodontal and preventative dentistry. She holds the strong belief that a healthy smile is the key to overall wellbeing. She gets great satisfaction in restoring aesthetics and function to a broken tooth, as well as educating her patients on preventative measures for the future. Priya ensures a safe, friendly and relaxed environment in which she is fully committed to ensuring the best quality dentistry.

  • Dr Elizabeth Im

    Dentist, Female

    Dr Elizabeth Im graduated from Kings College London university in the UK in 2009, with a Bachelor's degree in Dental Surgery. After graduating, Dr Im spent her first year in the public sector of community dental in the UK, where she treated many anxious and medically compromised patients, sometimes with the adjunct of inhalation sedation. In Dr Im's second year, she worked in hospital as a Senior House officer in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery. Dr Im then migrated here to Australia where she has mainly worked in the private sector, but has also been a mentor for dental students in James Cook University in Cairns. Dr Im is passionate about all aspects of dentistry, and has a great interest in oral surgery and cosmetic dentistry, especially with the use of CAD-CAM technology to produce same day crowns and ceramic fillings. You will find Dr Im has a caring and gentle nature combined with having a high regard for attention to detail, which ensures dedication in providing the most comprehensive care and pleasant treatment experience for all patients. Outside of dentistry Dr Im has a great passion for photography, international travel and trying out different cuisines that Brisbane has to offer.
