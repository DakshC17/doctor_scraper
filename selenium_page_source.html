About us

Centre For Human Potential (CFHP) is a psychologist practice that provide compassionate and attentive assistance to assist our clients in addressing their present challenges and enhancing their overall happiness and life satisfaction.

At our psychology clinic, we take a collaborative approach that fosters understanding, non-judgment, and confidentiality. Our dedicated team of psychologists is committed to helping you uncover your true values and goals, guiding you towards a more fulfilling life.

We understand that seeking therapy can be a challenging decision, which is why our psychologists are here to address all your questions and concerns. With their expertise, they will ensure you receive the appropriate type of therapy tailored to your unique needs, so you can embark on a transformative healing process.

We provide NDIS counselling services.

Booking services:

In order to schedule an appointment with a psychologist, please complete the enquiry form - Contact Practice. We can then contact you with the psychologist who best meets your needs based on this information. Our team will contact you within the next business day to finalise your booking.

Centre for Human Potential - Brisbane

Centre For Human Potential (CFHP) is a psychologist practice that provide compassionate and attentive assistance to assist our clients in addressing their present challenges and enhancing their overall happiness and life satisfaction.

At our psychology clinic, we take a collaborative approach that fosters understanding, non-judgment, and confidentiality. Our dedicated team of psychologists is committed to helping you uncover your true values and goals, guiding you towards a more fulfilling life.
